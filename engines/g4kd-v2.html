Common Failures and Inspection Risk Areas

AsiaPower does not currently have a verified G4KD repair-failure dataset in the repository. The V2 template should still help buyers inspect used engines by listing risk areas to verify before quotation and payment.

Noise and bottom-end check

Ask for startup video or running evidence where available. Any abnormal knocking must be reviewed before quote confirmation.

Overheating history

Check coolant condition, oil contamination signs, external leaks and donor vehicle condition before accepting the offer.

Compression and oil condition

For higher-value orders, request compression evidence or supplier condition statement if available.

Accessory mismatch

Confirm alternator, compressor, starter, mounts, harness and ECU requirements when comparing bare engine and half-cut offers.

These are inspection risk areas, not verified official G4KD failure claims. Each actual unit must be checked by APInventory before APSales quotes the buyer.

G4KD Buyer Guide

Before asking for price

Send model, year, gearbox, destination port and required buying format. A message that only says "G4KD price" is not enough for a reliable quote.

Before comparing offers

Separate bare engine, complete engine, engine + gearbox and half-cut offers. A cheaper offer may exclude accessories, ECU, harness, mounts or gearbox.

Before payment

Confirm photos, included parts, condition, packing plan and whether the quote is EXW or CIF. AsiaPower should not treat an inventory signal as confirmed stock until APInventory verifies it.

FAQ

Can AsiaPower quote G4KD now?

Yes, after APInventory confirms current supply, included parts, condition and price.

Is G4KD always the same across Hyundai and Kia?

No. Donor model, year, gearbox, ECU, harness and accessories still need confirmation.

Should I buy half-cut instead of engine only?

Choose half-cut when you need donor proof, wiring, ECU, mounts, accessories or gearbox context.

What port details should I send?

Send destination country and port such as Tema, Lagos, Cotonou, Douala, Abidjan or Mombasa.
