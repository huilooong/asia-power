Every vehicle carries a family's livelihood.

In many parts of the world, a car is more than transport. It may be a taxi driver's family income, a logistics company's production tool, or a young entrepreneur's first vehicle.

When a vehicle stops because one part is missing, the impact is rarely just one car — it is a family, a job, even a way of life.

We believe every engine, gearbox and component that can still be used deserves a second life — and every reliable transaction should be built on truth, transparency and trust.

Why we founded AsiaPower

For more than a decade we have worked in the automotive aftermarket. We have seen quality parts sold cheaply because information was opaque — and buyers take on heavy risk because reliable data was missing.

Suppliers struggle to reach global customers; inventory value stays locked.
Buyers struggle to judge fitment, source, quality and compatibility risk.
The industry still relies on experience, relationships and repeated back-and-forth to close deals.

We realised the missing piece was not parts — it was a digital platform connecting global suppliers, buyers, data and trust. That is why AsiaPower exists.

What we are building

AsiaPower is not only selling engines or gearboxes. We are building an AI-powered global circular economy platform.

With AI, VIN data, digital inventory, standardised inspection, global logistics and trusted trade, we want every reusable vehicle, part and industrial asset to be identified accurately, moved efficiently and traded with confidence.
