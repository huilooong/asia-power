About AsiaPower

Professional B2B export of premium used engines and gearboxes — bridging Asia's automotive supply chain with Africa's growing demand.

Our Story

Asia's Powertrain Export Partner for Africa

AsiaPower was founded on a simple principle: African importers, workshops, and fleet operators deserve the same access to quality used powertrains that established markets enjoy — with professional inspection, transparent pricing, and reliable logistics.

Operating from our headquarters in Zhengzhou, China and our West African office in Accra, Ghana, we maintain direct relationships with over 200 verified dismantling yards and export specialists across Japan, Korea, and China. Every engine and gearbox we ship undergoes rigorous quality inspection before export.

We serve B2B clients across 25+ African countries — from single-unit workshop orders to full container shipments for fleet replacement programs.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Quality First

Every unit is compression tested, leak checked, and documented with photo and video reports. We reject substandard inventory — our reputation depends on what arrives at your port.

Transparent Trade

Clear FOB/CIF pricing, honest condition grading, and no hidden fees. B2B clients receive detailed quotations with shipping options and warranty terms upfront.

Long-Term Partnerships

We invest in relationships — with African importers who depend on consistent supply, and with Asian suppliers who trust us to represent their inventory professionally.

Our Journey

Company Milestones
