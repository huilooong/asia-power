Truck Sourcing FAQ

What truck brands do you stock?

We regularly stock HINO (Dutro, Ranger, Profia), ISUZU (Elf, Forward, Giga), Mitsubishi Fuso (Canter, Fighter, Super Great), UD Trucks (Condor, Quon), Dongfeng, FAW Jiefang, Foton Auman and Sinotruk HOWO. Custom sourcing is available for any Japanese, Korean or Chinese brand not listed.

Do you sell complete trucks or only parts?

Both. We supply complete used trucks (tractor heads, tippers, cargo), half-cuts with full powertrain, front cab assemblies, and dismantled engine + gearbox sets. Specify what you need and we'll quote accordingly.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Single units via LCL are accepted. Container loads (20ft or 40ft) receive preferential pricing. We handle both one-off procurement and repeat bulk orders for fleet operators and importers.

Which markets do you export truck inventory to?

Primarily Sub-Saharan Africa (Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Mozambique), Southeast Asia (Thailand, Vietnam, Myanmar, Philippines) and Central Asia. We quote FOB Zhengzhou / CIF destination port.
