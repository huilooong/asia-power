Article 2 — Price & Payment 第二条 · 价格与付款
  1. Total contract value as per Article 1, currency USD.合同总金额见第一条，币种为 USD。
  2. Payment terms: 30% deposit by telegraphic transfer (T/T) upon contract signing; balance 70% before shipment.付款方式：合同签订后电汇（T/T）支付 30% 定金；发货前付清余款 70%。
  3. All bank charges on buyer's side shall be borne by Party B; charges on seller's side by Party A.买方侧银行费用由乙方承担；卖方侧银行费用由甲方承担。
Article 3 — Delivery & Shipping 第三条 · 交货与运输
  1. Delivery time: within 15–25 working days after receipt of deposit and confirmation of specifications.交货期：收到定金并确认规格后 15–25 个工作日内。
  2. Port of loading: Qingdao, China; Port of discharge: as designated by Party B.装运港：中国青岛；目的港：由乙方指定。
  3. Party A shall provide commercial invoice, packing list, and bill of lading (or equivalent shipping documents).甲方须提供商业发票、装箱单及提单（或等效运输单据）。
  4. Risk transfers to Party B according to the agreed Incoterms.货物风险按约定的贸易术语转移至乙方。
Article 4 — Inspection & Quality 第四条 · 检验与质量
  1. Goods shall match the description in Article 1. Party A provides whole-vehicle startup video before dismantling when applicable, inspection photos/video and pre-shipment verification records.货物须符合第一条描述。甲方在适用时提供拆解前整车启动视频、验货照片/视频及出运前验证记录。
  2. Party B shall inspect goods within 7 days of arrival at destination port and notify Party A in writing of any claim supported by evidence.乙方须于货物到港后 7 日内检验，如有异议须书面通知甲方并提供证据。
  3. Warranty: 30 days from arrival for internal engine components, subject to professional installation and normal use. Excludes external accessories, electrical items, and damage caused by improper installation or operation.质保：到港后 30 天（发动机内部件，须专业安装及正常使用）。不含外部附件、电器件及因安装或操作不当造成的损坏。
Article 5 — Force Majeure 第五条 · 不可抗力

Neither Party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, war, government restrictions, or port congestion. The affected Party shall notify the other within 7 days and resume performance when possible.因不可抗力（含自然灾害、战争、政府限制、港口拥堵等）导致无法履约的，受影响方不承担违约责任，但须在 7 日内通知对方，并在可能时恢复履行。

Article 6 — Dispute Resolution 第六条 · 争议解决

Disputes shall first be resolved through friendly negotiation. If unresolved within 30 days, either Party may submit the dispute to China International Economic and Trade Arbitration Commission (CIETAC), with arbitration seat in Beijing, China. The award shall be final and binding.争议应首先友好协商；30 日内未解决的，任何一方可提交 中国国际经济贸易仲裁委员会（CIETAC） 仲裁，仲裁地 中国北京，裁决为终局裁决。

Article 7 — General 第七条 · 其他
  1. This Contract is executed in English and Chinese. In case of discrepancy, the English version prevails unless otherwise agreed.本合同以中英文书写；如有歧义，除非另有约定，以英文版本为准。
  2. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.任何修改须书面形式并经双方签字确认。
  3. This Contract becomes effective upon signature by both Parties.本合同自双方签字之日起生效。
