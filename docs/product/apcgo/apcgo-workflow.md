# APCGO v1 Workflow

## Daily Workflow

APCGO follows this operating sequence:

```text
Input
  ↓
Analysis
  ↓
Opportunity Discovery
  ↓
Priority Ranking
  ↓
Executive Recommendation
  ↓
Human Approval
  ↓
APSales / Content / Operations
```

## 1. Input

APCGO collects approved signals from:

- existing AsiaPower pages
- engine page inventory
- public business information
- public competitor pages
- keyword research
- analytics and Search Console when available
- internal growth reports
- market notes
- inventory demand signals

Rules:

- public data only for company discovery
- no private data collection
- no customer contact
- no automatic publishing

## 2. Analysis

APCGO analyzes inputs by module:

- Lead Intelligence checks company fit.
- Content Intelligence checks search and inquiry potential.
- Competitor Intelligence checks ranking gaps.
- Market Intelligence checks country/category demand.
- Distribution Intelligence checks approved publishing channels.
- Executive Intelligence converts signals into actions.

Analysis questions:

- Is this opportunity real?
- Can AsiaPower serve it?
- Could it create traffic?
- Could it create leads?
- Could it create inquiries?
- Is action safe?
- Who should own it?

## 3. Opportunity Discovery

APCGO discovers opportunities in six categories.

### Qualified Companies

Daily target:

```text
100 qualified companies
```

Required fields:

- company
- country
- city
- website
- public email if available
- public phone/WhatsApp if business-published
- business type
- why valuable
- priority
- source

### Content Opportunities

Daily target:

```text
20 content opportunities
```

Required fields:

- target keyword
- search intent
- estimated business value
- expected inquiry value
- priority
- recommended page type
- next action

### High-Value Keywords

Output:

- engine keywords
- vehicle keywords
- country keywords
- comparison keywords
- buyer question keywords
- export/import keywords

### Competitor Opportunities

Output only actionable findings:

- missing pages
- weak competitor pages
- keywords competitors rank for
- new categories
- new market opportunities

### Market Opportunities

Output:

- country demand
- product category demand
- vehicle/engine demand
- emerging buyer questions

### Distribution Opportunities

Output:

- where approved content should be shared
- why the channel matters
- what content format fits
- approval required

## 4. Priority Ranking

APCGO ranks every opportunity.

Scoring dimensions:

- traffic potential
- qualified company value
- expected inquiry value
- buyer intent
- market fit
- supply fit
- competitor weakness
- speed to execute
- risk

Priority levels:

```text
S = execute today if approved
A = execute this week
B = batch or monitor
C = no action now
```

APCGO must not treat all opportunities equally.

The value of APCGO is prioritization.

## 5. Executive Recommendation

APCGO produces one executive recommendation set per day.

It must answer:

```text
What are the five highest-value actions AsiaPower should execute today to gain more traffic, leads, and inquiries?
```

Each action must include:

- recommended action
- expected KPI impact
- owner
- required approval
- reason for priority
- risk

## 6. Human Approval

APCGO stops before external execution.

Human approval is required for:

- contacting leads
- sending emails
- sending WhatsApp
- publishing content
- posting to social media
- deploying pages
- changing production website

APCGO may prepare drafts and instructions, but humans decide execution.

## 7. Handoff to APSales

APCGO hands opportunities to APSales only after:

- human approval
- a lead is selected for outreach
- an inquiry is generated
- a campaign is approved

APCGO handoff package:

- company or inquiry context
- source
- likely buyer need
- relevant AsiaPower product
- suggested first question
- priority
- risk notes

APSales owns:

- contact
- conversation
- qualification
- quote
- follow-up
- close

## 8. Handoff to APInventory

APCGO may send demand signals to APInventory.

Examples:

- high-demand engine codes
- country-specific demand
- competitor product categories
- repeated buyer questions

APInventory owns:

- stock confirmation
- supplier follow-up
- verified inventory
- assisted inventory
- supply intelligence

## 9. Daily Output Standard

Daily APCGO output should include:

- qualified company list
- content opportunity list
- high-value keyword list
- competitor opportunity list
- market opportunity notes
- distribution opportunity recommendations
- executive top five actions

Reports are not the KPI.

The KPI is the opportunity value created by these outputs.

## 10. Stop Rule

After producing recommendations, APCGO stops.

It waits for CTO or human approval before external execution.

No automatic outreach.
No automatic publishing.
No automatic deployment.
