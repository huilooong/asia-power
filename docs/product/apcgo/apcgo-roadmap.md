# APCGO v1 Roadmap

## Roadmap Overview

APCGO evolves through four stages:

```text
MVP
  ↓
Growth Intelligence
  ↓
Search Intelligence
  ↓
Market Intelligence
  ↓
Autonomous Growth Engine
```

Autonomous does not mean unapproved external action.

Even in the final stage:

```text
Human approval remains required before contacting, publishing, or deploying.
```

## Phase 1: MVP

Goal:

Produce useful daily opportunity outputs.

Capabilities:

- 100 qualified public companies per day
- 20 content opportunities per day
- competitor opportunities
- distribution opportunities
- daily top five executive actions

Primary outputs:

- qualified company lists
- content opportunities
- competitor gap list
- executive summary

Success criteria:

- outputs are specific
- opportunities are prioritized
- APSales can use approved leads
- leadership can choose daily actions

Limitations:

- manual or semi-manual research
- estimates may be directional
- no deep analytics integration

## Phase 2: Growth Intelligence

Goal:

Turn MVP outputs into a structured growth intelligence system.

Capabilities:

- opportunity database
- priority scoring
- repeatable lead qualification
- content opportunity scoring
- competitor gap categorization
- daily trend tracking

Modules:

- Lead Intelligence
- Content Intelligence
- Competitor Intelligence
- Distribution Intelligence
- Executive Intelligence

Success criteria:

- leadership can see opportunity pipeline
- duplicate opportunities are reduced
- high-value opportunities are tracked over time
- handoff to APSales becomes clearer

## Phase 3: Search Intelligence

Goal:

Connect APCGO with real search performance.

Future integrations:

- Google Search Console
- Google Analytics
- page index status
- keyword impressions
- CTR
- ranking range
- inquiry source tracking

Capabilities:

- detect low-CTR pages
- find keywords ranking positions 8-20
- recommend title/meta improvements
- detect pages with traffic but no inquiries
- prioritize content updates by search value

Success criteria:

- content decisions become data-backed
- engine pages improve based on actual search demand
- APCGO can estimate traffic opportunity more accurately

## Phase 4: Market Intelligence

Goal:

Connect search demand, public lead demand, competitor signals, and inventory signals.

Capabilities:

- country opportunity maps
- engine demand maps
- vehicle demand maps
- competitor market movement tracking
- demand feedback to APInventory
- market-specific content and lead recommendations

Examples:

- Ghana demand for Hyundai/Kia engines
- Nigeria demand for Toyota diesel engines
- UAE demand for half-cuts and export suppliers
- East Africa demand for truck parts

Success criteria:

- AsiaPower knows which markets and engine families to prioritize
- APInventory can use demand signals for supply focus
- APSales receives more relevant opportunity context

## Phase 5: Autonomous Growth Engine

Goal:

APCGO becomes a continuous opportunity engine.

Autonomous functions:

- continuously monitor keywords
- continuously monitor public leads
- continuously monitor competitors
- continuously recommend content
- continuously rank opportunities
- continuously prepare executive recommendations

Still requires human approval for:

- outreach
- emails
- WhatsApp
- publishing
- deployment
- production changes

Success criteria:

- daily opportunity generation is consistent
- growth backlog is always prioritized
- APSales receives better opportunities
- website content roadmap is demand-driven

- qualified companies discovered
- content opportunities discovered
- competitor opportunities discovered
- distribution opportunities recommended

- S/A priority opportunities created
- duplicate opportunity reduction
- approved opportunities

- inquiry-producing opportunities

### Search Intelligence KPIs

- high-value keywords discovered
- estimated traffic opportunity
- pages improved from Search Console signals
- impression growth
- CTR improvement opportunities

### Market Intelligence KPIs

- market opportunities identified
- country opportunity maps
- demand signals passed to APInventory
- engine families prioritized

### Autonomous Growth Engine KPIs

- recurring opportunity generation
- approved action rate
- inquiry value influenced
- traffic value influenced
- APSales pipeline contribution

## Governance Roadmap

Every phase must preserve:

- no unapproved outreach
- no unapproved publishing
- no unapproved deployment
- public-data-only lead discovery
- no private supplier exposure
- no fake inventory claims
- no fake SEO claims
