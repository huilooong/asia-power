# GROWTH-001 Global Scrap Parts Traffic Mission

Commander: Kongming / OpenClaw brain
Requested by: CEO
Date: 2026-07-05 Africa/Accra

## CEO Need

AsiaPower needs its website and product information to be seen by global used auto parts, scrap parts, dismantling yard, salvage yard, auto parts importer, wholesaler, and repair workshop buyers, especially in Africa.

The goal is not vanity content. The goal is:

- more relevant people seeing AsiaPower
- more qualified visits to `asia-power.com`
- more buyer inquiries
- more repeatable outreach/distribution systems run by agents

## Target Audiences

- Used engine importers
- Used auto parts importers
- Dismantling yards / scrap yards / salvage yards
- Auto parts wholesalers
- Repair workshops
- Fleet maintenance companies
- Truck and commercial vehicle workshops
- Dealers who source half-cuts, engines, gearboxes, front cuts, and chassis parts

Priority regions:

1. Africa, especially Ghana, Nigeria, Kenya, Tanzania, and surrounding markets
2. UAE / Dubai used parts trade hub
3. Middle East and global re-export markets
4. Other countries where Japanese/Korean/Chinese used parts demand exists

## Constraints

- Do not send emails, WhatsApp messages, DMs, or social posts automatically.
- Do not scrape private personal data.
- Do not claim verified stock that is not verified.
- Do not invent official engine specs.
- Do not create broad code changes before strategy is approved.
- Separate traffic strategy from production deployment.

## Required Strategy Areas

Agents should propose a practical system covering:

1. Direct public lead discovery and approved outreach
2. SEO pages for engine/country/vehicle/half-cut intent
3. Directory and marketplace distribution
4. Social/content distribution without spam
5. Partner and supplier acquisition
6. Website conversion path from visitor to inquiry
7. Tracking: visits, source, lead quality, inquiries, conversion
8. Agent ownership: APBD, APCGO, APSEO, APSales, APCOO

Every proposal must answer:

- What can be done this week?
- What can be automated safely?
