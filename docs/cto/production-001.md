## Risk Notes

- These are V1 SEO pages. They are intentionally conservative and may need manual enrichment before aggressive promotion.
- The pages do not claim live stock availability. Related half-cut records are described as records that require confirmation before quote.
- Supplier names, supplier phone numbers, private notes, full VINs, and private chat-derived intelligence are not exposed.
- Official engine specifications are not invented. Missing official specs display `Not verified yet`.
- Some engine codes such as Mercedes numeric variants use normalized URL slugs like `651-955.html`; redirects may be needed later if canonical naming changes.
- Existing brand-prefixed engine pages were not rewritten by this batch; this creates parallel code-first SEO URLs that should later be reconciled with canonical strategy.
