Common Failures and Inspection Risk Areas

The preview uses responsible language: these are inspection risk areas, not unverified official failure claims.

Noise and bottom-end check

Ask for startup video or running evidence where available. Abnormal knocking blocks quotation until reviewed.

Overheating evidence

Check coolant marks, oil contamination, visible leaks and donor vehicle condition.

Compression confidence

For high-value orders, request compression evidence or supplier condition statement where possible.

Accessory mismatch

Confirm mounts, harness, ECU, alternator, compressor and starter before comparing prices.
