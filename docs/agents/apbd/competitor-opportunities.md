# APBD Competitor Opportunities

## Purpose

APBD monitors competitors to find opportunities AsiaPower can act on.

The output must be actionable.

APBD should not produce competitor summaries unless they lead to a traffic, lead, or inquiry opportunity.

## What to Monitor

APBD should watch competitors for:

- new engine pages
- new vehicle pages
- new country landing pages
- new buying guides
- new FAQ pages
- new comparison pages
- new product categories
- new ranking keywords
- weak pages AsiaPower can outperform
- missing content AsiaPower can build

## Actionable Finding Types

### Missing AsiaPower Content

Competitor has a page or topic AsiaPower lacks.

Required output:

- competitor page/topic
- AsiaPower missing page
- recommended page type
- priority

### Weak Competitor Page

Competitor ranks with thin or weak content.

Required output:

- competitor page
- weakness
- recommended AsiaPower response
- expected traffic/inquiry value

### Keyword Opportunity

Competitor appears for a keyword AsiaPower should target.

Required output:

- keyword
- search intent
- competitor
- recommended AsiaPower page
- priority

### New Market Opportunity

Competitor targets a country or category AsiaPower can serve.

Required output:

- market/category
- competitor move
- AsiaPower opportunity
- suggested action

## Required Fields

Each competitor opportunity must include:

```text
Competitor
Observed opportunity
Why it matters
Recommended AsiaPower action
Expected business value
Priority
Source
```

## Priority Levels

S:

High traffic or high buyer intent opportunity that AsiaPower can act on quickly.

A:

Useful opportunity requiring content work or validation.

B:

Monitor or batch for later.

## Daily Output Format

Daily competitor files should use:

```text
docs/agents/apbd/daily-competitor-opportunities-YYYY-MM-DD.md
```

Recommended table:

```text
| Competitor | Opportunity | Why It Matters | Recommended AsiaPower Action | Business Value | Priority | Source |
```

## Rules

APBD must not:

- attack competitors publicly
- scrape private competitor systems
- copy competitor content
- publish automatically
- change AsiaPower pages automatically

APBD should:

- identify gaps
- recommend better content
- prioritize buyer intent
- connect findings to traffic, leads, and inquiries

## Example Finding

```text
Competitor: Example Auto Parts Exporter
Opportunity: ranks with a thin "Toyota 2TR engine" page
Why it matters: 2TR-FE is a high-value engine for Hilux/Hiace markets
Recommended action: create or improve AsiaPower 2TR-FE Engine Intelligence page with half-cut and gearbox sections
Business value: high
Priority: S
Source: public competitor page
```
