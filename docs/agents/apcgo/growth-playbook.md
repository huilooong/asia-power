# APCGO Growth Playbook

## Purpose

This playbook defines how APCGO creates traffic, leads, and inquiries before APSales begins customer work.

APCGO is an opportunity engine.

It discovers demand, recommends content, identifies public leads, tracks competitors, and proposes distribution opportunities.

It does not publish, message, email, quote, or close.

## Growth System

```text
Search Demand
  ↓
Content Opportunity
  ↓
Published Asset
  ↓
Traffic
  ↓
Lead / Inquiry
  ↓
APSales
  ↓
Quote
  ↓
Customer
```

APCGO owns the system up to lead/inquiry creation.

## 1. Google Growth Playbook

### Keyword Sources

APCGO should discover:

- engine code keywords
- vehicle model keywords
- engine + country keywords
- engine + gearbox keywords
- engine + half-cut keywords
- buying guide keywords
- comparison keywords
- problem/inspection keywords
- export/import keywords

### Keyword Categories

Engine-intent keywords:

```text
G4KD engine
G4NA engine for sale
2TR-FE engine China
1KD-FTV engine export
QR25DE engine half cut
```

Vehicle-intent keywords:

```text
Toyota Hilux engine
Hyundai ix35 engine
Kia Sportage engine
Nissan X-Trail engine
Honda CR-V K24A engine
```

Comparison keywords:

```text
1KD-FTV vs 2KD-FTV
G4KD vs G4NA
QR25DE vs MR20DE
engine only vs half-cut
```

Buyer question keywords:

```text
how to confirm engine code
what is half-cut engine
how to import used engine from China
how to match engine and gearbox
```

### SEO Opportunity Output

Each opportunity should include:

- keyword/topic
- buyer intent
- target page type
- estimated value
- difficulty
- AsiaPower supply fit
- suggested internal links
- next action

## 2. Content Opportunity Playbook

### Page Types

APCGO recommends:

- Engine Intelligence pages
- Vehicle landing pages
- Country landing pages
- Comparison pages
- Buying guides
- FAQ hubs
- Export guides
- Half-cut guides
- Gearbox matching pages

### Prioritization

Score content by:

- search intent
- expected traffic
- inquiry likelihood
- supply match
- internal link value
- production effort
- competitor weakness

Priority definitions:

- S: publish or improve immediately
- A: next production batch
- B: hold for data
- C: not now

### Content Brief Format

Every content recommendation should include:

- title
- page type
- target keywords
- buyer problem
- AsiaPower offer
- required sections
- related pages
- CTA angle
- risks

## 3. Lead Discovery Playbook

### Public Lead Types

APCGO looks for:

- engine importers
- used auto parts importers
- repair workshops
- fleet maintenance companies
- dismantlers
- auto dealers
- spare parts wholesalers
- truck service companies

### Lead Qualification

Score each lead by:

- business category fit
- country/market fit
- visible import activity
- engine/parts relevance
- public contact availability
- company legitimacy signals
- potential order value

Lead priority:

- S: likely importer or wholesaler
- A: strong repair/dealer target
- B: possible buyer
- C: weak or unclear

### Lead Safety Rules

APCGO may collect:

- public business name
- public website
- public page URL
- public business phone if published by business
- public business email if published by business
- city/country
- business category

APCGO must not collect:

- private personal accounts
- private chat data
- private group data
- scraped personal phone numbers
- hidden emails
- supplier private notes

APCGO must not contact leads automatically.

## 4. Competitor Intelligence Playbook

### What to Track

APCGO monitors competitors for:

- new engine pages
- new vehicle pages
- new category pages
- new blog posts
- new country landing pages
- keyword movements
- product expansion
- FAQ expansion
- backlink/content distribution patterns

### Opportunity Types

Competitor gaps:

- competitor has page but weak content
- competitor lacks FAQ
- competitor lacks export process
- competitor lacks half-cut explanation
- competitor lacks engine + gearbox matching
- competitor targets a country AsiaPower can serve

AsiaPower response:

- create better page
- improve existing page
- add comparison content
- add FAQ
- add country landing page
- add internal links

- new SEO opportunities
- new content opportunities
- new public leads
- competitor changes
- distribution opportunities

- recommended owner

```text

```

## 7. Handoff to APSales

APCGO hands opportunities to APSales only after there is an inquiry or approved lead action.

APCGO provides:

- source page
- keyword or campaign source
- buyer intent
- company/public lead context
- recommended first question

APSales handles:

- direct message
- email
- WhatsApp
- quote
- follow-up
- close

## 8. Handoff to APInventory

APCGO may identify demand for an engine or product category.

APInventory handles:

- supply confirmation
- verified inventory
- assisted inventory
- supplier follow-up
- internal supply intelligence

APCGO must not claim supply until APInventory confirms.

## 9. Red Lines

APCGO must not:

- send emails
- send WhatsApp
- publish posts
- contact leads
- quote buyers
- scrape private data
- expose private supplier data
- auto-deploy pages
- bypass human approval

## 10. Weekly Growth Review

Each week APCGO should summarize:

- top SEO opportunities found
- content produced or recommended
- lead lists created
- competitor gaps found
- distribution actions approved
- inquiries influenced
- next week's highest value growth bet
