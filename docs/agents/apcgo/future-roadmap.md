# APCGO Future Roadmap

## Purpose

This roadmap defines how AsiaPower can evolve APCGO from a manual report-generating growth officer into a structured AI growth operating system.

The goal remains unchanged:

```text
Generate more traffic.
Generate more leads.
Generate more inquiries.
```

APCGO must remain before APSales.

APCGO creates opportunity.
APSales converts opportunity.

## Phase 1: Manual Intelligence Reports

Status:

```text
Design stage
```

Capabilities:

- daily SEO opportunity list
- content opportunity list
- public lead discovery report
- competitor intelligence report
- distribution opportunity report
- daily growth dashboard

Human approval:

- required for all external actions

Success criteria:

- APCGO can produce useful daily reports
- leadership can choose a highest priority growth action
- APSales receives better-qualified opportunities

## Phase 2: Structured Opportunity Database

Goal:

Move from markdown-only reporting to structured opportunity records.

Opportunity types:

- SEO keyword
- content idea
- public lead
- competitor gap
- distribution channel
- country opportunity
- engine opportunity
- vehicle opportunity

Required fields:

- id
- type
- title
- source
- market
- priority
- expected traffic value
- lead potential
- status
- owner
- next action
- created date
- last updated

Benefits:

- easier prioritization
- easier follow-up
- trend tracking
- reporting consistency

## Phase 3: Search Console and Analytics Integration

Goal:

Connect APCGO to actual performance data.

Data sources:

- Google Search Console
- Google Analytics
- server logs where available
- inquiry source tracking

APCGO should identify:

- pages gaining impressions
- pages with low CTR
- keywords ranking 8-20
- pages needing better title/meta
- pages generating inquiries
- pages with traffic but no conversion

Human approval remains required for content changes.

## Phase 4: Engine Opportunity Ranking Integration

Goal:

APCGO should connect SEO demand with engine commercial value.

Signals:

- China supply
- Africa demand
- historical inquiries
- inventory signals
- Google opportunity
- profit potential
- competition difficulty

Outputs:

- engine page priority list
- content refresh priority
- new page generation candidates
- manual optimization candidates

## Phase 5: Public Lead Discovery Automation

Goal:

APCGO generates qualified public lead lists at scale.

Allowed:

- public business websites
- public business directories
- public Google Maps listings
- public company pages

Not allowed:

- private groups
- private messages
- hidden personal data
- auto-contact

Output:

- scored public business lead lists
- lead category
- country
- reason for qualification
- suggested human-approved outreach angle

## Phase 6: Competitor Monitoring System

Goal:

Track competitors continuously.

Signals:

- new pages
- changed titles
- new categories
- new products
- new country pages
- content gaps
- ranking movements

Outputs:

- weekly competitor digest
- urgent opportunity alerts
- recommended AsiaPower response

## Phase 7: Content Brief Generator

Goal:

Turn growth opportunities into developer/operator-ready briefs.

Brief types:

- Engine Intelligence page
- vehicle page
- comparison page
- FAQ page
- country landing page
- buying guide
- social content draft
- video outline

Each brief should include:

- target keyword
- buyer intent
- page goal
- required sections
- internal links
- CTA
- data sources needed
- risk notes

No content is published automatically.

## Phase 8: APSales Handoff Intelligence

Goal:

When a lead or inquiry is generated, APCGO enriches the handoff to APSales.

Handoff data:

- source page
- likely keyword intent
- country
- buyer category
- recommended first question
- related engine/vehicle context
- suggested product path

APSales remains responsible for customer communication.

## Phase 9: APInventory Demand Feedback

Goal:

APCGO helps APInventory understand demand.

Demand signals:

- search growth
- new country interest
- repeated engine searches
- competitor product focus
- inquiry patterns

APInventory can use this to:

- prioritize supply confirmation
- ask suppliers for high-demand engines
- validate assisted inventory
- watch supply intelligence areas

## Phase 10: Approval-Based Distribution Assistant

Goal:

APCGO recommends content distribution packages.

Package types:

- LinkedIn post draft
- Facebook post draft
- YouTube video outline
- forum answer draft
- blog syndication suggestion
- country-specific content plan

Rules:

- no automatic publishing
- no automatic replies
- no automatic DMs
- human approval required

## Phase 11: Growth Dashboard

Goal:

Create an executive dashboard for APCGO.

Dashboard sections:

- traffic opportunities
- keyword opportunities
- lead opportunities
- competitor opportunities
- distribution opportunities
- content pipeline
- highest priority action today
- weekly trend

Dashboard question:

```text
What should AsiaPower do next to create more traffic, leads, and inquiries?
```

## Phase 12: AI Growth Operating System

Long-term vision:

APCGO becomes the AI operating layer for growth.

It connects:

- SEO
- content
- lead discovery
- competitor intelligence
- distribution planning
- APSales handoff
- APInventory demand feedback
- growth reporting

It does not replace humans.
It does not contact customers automatically.
It does not publish automatically.

It makes AsiaPower faster at finding and acting on growth opportunities.

## Governance

Permanent rules:

- no emails without approval
- no WhatsApp without approval
- no social publishing without approval
- no private data collection
- no supplier privacy exposure
- no fake inventory claims
- no fake SEO claims
- no production deployment without approval
