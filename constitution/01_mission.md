# Mission

## 中文

构建全球最值得信赖的循环资产供应链平台，通过 AI 和数字化技术，让每一件仍有价值的设备，在最需要它的地方继续创造价值，为更多家庭、企业和社会降低成本、创造机会、改善生活。

## English

Build the world's most trusted circular asset supply chain platform, using AI and digital technology to help every valuable machine continue creating value where it is needed most, reducing costs, creating opportunities, and improving lives for families, businesses, and societies.
